About RDP Auto

West Yarmouth's Go-To Mobile Mechanic

RDP Auto & Small Engine is a locally owned and operated mobile mechanic business serving the Cape Cod community. We built this company on a simple promise: honest work, fair prices, and the convenience of coming right to your door.

Unlike big shops, we don't push unnecessary repairs or charge you for things you don't need. We explain everything before we start, and you approve the quote — always.
